# MIYAKOYA Logo Generation Prompt

## Brand Overview

Brand Name: MIYAKOYA  
Category: Ballet Costume / Atelier Brand  
Positioning: Premium, Elegant, Modern Tradition  
Concept: One-of-a-kind, fully custom-made ballet costumes that support dancers’ dreams.  
Target: Ballet dancers, parents of dancers, competition and stage performers.  
Tone & Mood: Elegant, refined, timeless, calm luxury (not flashy, not cute).

Color Preference:

- Monochrome (Black / White)
- Optional accent: muted gold or warm gray
  No bright colors.

Font Preference:

- Serif or refined modern serif
- High fashion / atelier feeling
- Clear readability, wide letter spacing
- Avoid playful or casual fonts

---

## Logo Version B: Secondary Logo (With Subline)

### Text

MIYAKOYA  
atelier ballet costume

※ Subline can be lowercase and smaller than main text.

### Purpose

- Supporting logo for footer, documents, introduction materials
- Helps first-time visitors understand the brand category

### Design Requirements

- Main logo text dominates
- Subline is subtle, refined, and unobtrusive
- Clean vertical or horizontal alignment
- Same font family or a perfectly matching secondary font
- Elegant spacing between logo and subline
- No excessive decoration

### Emotional Impression

- Professional
- Artistic craftsmanship
- Clear but still premium

---

## Output Requirements

- Transparent background
- PNG preferred (SVG optional if available)
- High resolution
- Clean edges
- Suitable for web and print use

---

## Notes

- This is a Japanese brand, but logo text is Roman alphabet only
- Avoid trends that will feel outdated
- Aim for a logo that still feels beautiful 10 years from now
